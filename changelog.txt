[B]Retrospect v5.2.8 - Changelog - 2020-07-09[/B]

A small release for Retrospect that includes a number of new Dutch regional channels and fixes some minor issues with some of the existing channels.

[B]Framework related[/B]
* Added: Option for post-processing of retrieved items

[B]GUI/Settings/Language related[/B]
_None_

[B]Channel related[/B]
* Added: WOS regional videos
* Added: DTV Nieuws regional videos
* Added: Omroep Venlo
* Added: Omroep Horst aan de Maas
* Added: Studio 040
* Fixed: Pathe.nl missing thumbs caused some trailers to not list
* Fixed: UR Play changed date format which broke parsing (Fixes #1403)
* Fixed: Cleanup the titles in UR Play for recent and popular lists
* Fixed: AT5 updated their API which broke listings (Fixes #1406)
